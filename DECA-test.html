    Membership

    Become a DECA member! Any person or family who lives within the DECA borders can join. DECA’s borders are Main Street (east), Lumsden/Mortimer (north), Monarch Park, (west) and the train tracks (south). A family membership costs just $10 per year per household. It not only gives your family the right to vote on how DECA is operated, but we also use that money to run our programs and it establishes our credibility at city hall.
